About Us

We are a team of volunteer from all around Campus Biotech. To contribute in building a more vibrant community, we are bringing many events to the campus: from our well known thematic Happy Hours (Halloween, karaoke, trivia...), the Synergy Lunches, to our French classes and improv courses

Our events can also come to life thanks to the support of our sponsors and partners
