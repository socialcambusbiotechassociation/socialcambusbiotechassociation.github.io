About Us

[2-3 sentences bio]
Eager to get a sense of community, members of the campus from both UNIGE and EPFL joined forces in 2016 to create the association.

Each year, a team of amazing volunteers come together to bring many events to the campus: from our now traditional monthly Happy Hours, most with a specific theme (Halloween experience, karaoke, trivia, Minute to win it games...), the Synergy Lunches, to our French classes and improv courses...

After a long pause due to covid, we are now back on track, eager to continue where we left off: with exciting events and activities to inspire and energize you and thus contributing in building a more vibrant community!
